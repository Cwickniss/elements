TECHCORP BRAND GUIDELINES 2024
===========================

1. Brand Identity
================

1.1 Mission Statement
-------------------
To empower businesses and individuals with innovative technology solutions that simplify their digital journey.

1.2 Brand Values
--------------
- Innovation
- Reliability
- Accessibility
- Security
- Customer Success

2. Visual Elements
=================

2.1 Logo Usage
-------------
Primary Logo Colors:
- Blue (#0066CC): Trust, Technology, Reliability
- Gray (#4A4A4A): Professional, Modern
- White (#FFFFFF): Clean, Simple

Logo Clear Space: Minimum 1x height of logo on all sides
Minimum Size: 32px height for digital, 1 inch for print

2.2 Color Palette
---------------
Primary Colors:
- TechBlue: #0066CC
- CharcoalGray: #4A4A4A
- CloudWhite: #FFFFFF

Secondary Colors:
- SuccessGreen: #28A745
- AlertOrange: #FD7E14
- ErrorRed: #DC3545
- InfoTeal: #17A2B8

2.3 Typography
------------
Primary Font: 
- Headings: Roboto Bold
- Body: Roboto Regular
- Technical: Roboto Mono

Web Safe Alternatives:
- Arial (sans-serif)
- Helvetica (sans-serif)

3. Brand Voice
=============

3.1 Personality
-------------
- Professional but friendly
- Knowledgeable but accessible
- Innovative but reliable
- Global but personal

3.2 Writing Style
---------------
- Clear and concise
- Solution-focused
- Technically accurate
- Empowering

4. Marketing Materials
====================

4.1 Social Media
--------------
Image Sizes:
- LinkedIn: 1200x627px
- Twitter: 1200x675px
- Facebook: 1200x630px
- Instagram: 1080x1080px

Content Types:
- Product updates
- Tech tips
- Customer success stories
- Industry insights
- Company culture

4.2 Email Marketing
-----------------
- Subject lines: 50 characters max
- Preview text: 100 characters
- Body width: 600px
- CTAs: Clear, action-oriented

4.3 Website
----------
- Responsive design
- Accessibility compliance
- Clear navigation
- Consistent spacing

5. Product Branding
==================

5.1 Product Names
---------------
Format: TechCorp [Product Category] [Version]
Example: TechCorp Analytics Pro 2024

5.2 Feature Names
---------------
Format: [Action]+ [Function]
Example: SmartSync, RapidDeploy

6. Content Guidelines
===================

6.1 Technical Content
-------------------
- Use clear explanations
- Include practical examples
- Progressive disclosure
- Visual aids when possible

6.2 Marketing Content
-------------------
- Focus on benefits
- Include social proof
- Clear call-to-action
- Measurable outcomes

7. Brand Assets
==============

7.1 Photography
-------------
Style:
- Clean and modern
- Natural lighting
- Diverse representation
- Professional setting

7.2 Icons
--------
Style:
- Minimal
- Consistent stroke width
- Rounded corners
- Single color

8. Legal Requirements
===================

8.1 Trademark Usage
-----------------
- ® for registered trademarks
- ™ for unregistered trademarks
- Required legal disclaimers

8.2 Privacy
----------
- GDPR compliance
- Data protection
- Cookie policies
- Privacy statements
